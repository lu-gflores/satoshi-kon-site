Renowned Works of Satoshi Kon

Satoshi Kon

1963 - 2010

Satoshi Kon attended Musahino Art University in Tokyo where he studied graphic design. He started his career as a managa artist and editor for Young Magazine and later made his debut with a short manga Toriko.Kon soon became an assistant to Katsuhior Otomo, the creator of the renowed anime Akira and went on to collaborate with several other animated films like Memories.

During this partnership, he continued his work in manga, creating Opus that ran between 1995 and 1996, up until 1997 where he began his directorial debut in Perfect Blue.
